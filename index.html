Hi, I'm Maria.

I am a nature photographer and bird enthusiast in Southwest Louisiana. I love to capture the plants, animals and birds of Calcasieu Parish and the places I visit. My favorites are Fred and Ethel, a mating pair of eastern bluebirds in my backyard. My photography has been featured in Southwest Louisiana's Thrive calendars and reposted by Birds & Blooms and Ontario Bluebird Society on Facebook and Instagram.
